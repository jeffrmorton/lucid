# Neurofeedback Protocols

YAML-defined neurofeedback training protocols. Each protocol specifies electrode montage, frequency bands, training parameters, and evidence level.

## Available Protocols

- **smr_training.yaml** — Sensorimotor rhythm (12-15 Hz) at Cz. Evidence: moderate for focus and sleep.
- **alpha_theta.yaml** — Alpha/theta training for relaxation. Evidence: moderate for anxiety.
- **beta_training.yaml** — Beta enhancement for sustained attention.

## Evidence Levels

Protocols are rated by clinical evidence strength:
- **strong** — Multiple sham-controlled RCTs with consistent positive results
- **moderate** — Some controlled trials with positive results
- **preliminary** — Pilot studies or uncontrolled trials
- **theoretical** — Plausible mechanism but limited evidence
